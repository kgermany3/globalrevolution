Introduction

Pedapp is an extension of poccils Essentials starter kit, which is itself an expansion on Flamegurus starter kit. The aim of Pedapp is to add features from the Generation IV games into the Essentials kit, as well as adding some things that may have been missing. The kit is designed to speed up the Pokémon fangame process, without taking away all of the creative input.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have you done the Battle animations?

No. The battle animations are not included in the project. It would take an immense amount of time to do and I just don't have that time, sorry.

What is the difference between the Basic and Complete Versions and why release it like this?

Well, the Complete version comes with all of the features (at least all of the ones that are done) whereas the Basic version has some of the additional features removed. The main reasoning for this was so that those who are skilled in RGSS and are scripting their own changes in can use the Complete version as a timesaver and not have to worry about adding all of the Gen IV stuff AND their own additions. The reason for having a Basic one as well is to encourage growth in the community. A lot of people just expect things to be handed to them for nothing. I'd love to be able to do that, but if everything is already laid out then what do you gaiun from your fangame? What skills do you learn? With some of the features missing from the Basic Version it will hopefully encourage people to learn a bit of Ruby and implement the missing things rather than just carry on with the missing elements.

Why isn't everything included?

There is a lot to add, plus I apparently have things to do in 'The Real World'. I figured that releasing what had been done and then updating in the same ay poccil does would be more useful than keeping it to myself until I'm 100% happy with it (which will probably be never). I'll be releasing updates as and when I get stuff done.

New Stuff

Tile Tags

There are a few new Tile Tags that can be assigned in Pedapp. Here they are

14:Puddle. Shows you a funky reflection but won't ask you to surf on it every time you press a button.

15:MediumSnow. Will slow you down a little bit, you can't use the bike or run in this snow.

16:DeepSnow.Takes foreeeeeeeeever. Can't run or bike.

17:Snow. You can still go at full speed, you just can't use the bike.

The Moves/Abilities

Most of the moves that have been added have been placed in the PokeBattle_MoveEffects script. If you want to take a look to try and figure out how they work then that's the place to look! the abilities are scattered all over the place but can be found by searching for the internal name (usually the ability name, without spaces and in capitals).

The Beauty Condition

Despite the fact that neither Pokéblocks or Poffins have been added yet Beauty, Toughness, Cool etc. have been put as attributes. To affect these attributes, check out the familiar looking kid in the second floor of the gatehouse.

The Plates/Adaptability

When Arceus holds a plate, both he and the move Judgment will take on the element of the plate. He'll also display the correct sprite... unless he's shiny. I'm lazy. So sue me.
